About Sukoon Calligraphy

Sukoon Calligraphy is a sanctuary where art meets passion. Founded by Girliee, an artist and calligrapher with years of experience, we specialize in bringing your visions to life through elegant sketches, unique paintings, and exquisite calligraphy.

Whether you’re looking for a personalized commission, decorative prints, or custom lettering for special occasions, Sukoon Calligraphy offers a variety of services tailored to your needs. Our mission is to create timeless pieces that resonate emotionally and visually.

  • Expertise in watercolor, ink, and digital art
  • Custom calligraphy for weddings, logos, and branding
  • Affordable pricing with attention to quality
